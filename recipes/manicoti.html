Cheese Stuffed Manicotti

Enjoy this delicious cheese-stuffed manicotti, filled with a savory blend of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheese, topped with marinara sauce, and baked to perfection. This dish is a cozy Italian classic that's sure to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • 12 manicotti shells
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Boil the manicotti shells according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan, and egg. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Stuff each manicotti shell with the cheese mixture.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce in the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Place the stuffed manicotti in the dish and cover with the remaining marinara sauce.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes to brown the top.
  8. Serve and enjoy!
